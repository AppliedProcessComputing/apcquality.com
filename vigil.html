Vigil

Built for local document control.

Vigil is a local-first desktop document control system for organizations that need to keep important business documents from being forgotten, expired, misplaced, or unmanaged.

Discuss Document Control Back to Products Home

Release in progress

Vigil is currently in development and being prepared for a future Windows release. It is not publicly available for purchase or download yet, but the product direction is set: serious local document control for small organizations that need operational records to stay visible and current without requiring a cloud service.

What Vigil tracks

Organizations and relationships

Accounts, vendors, providers, owners, and the records connected to them.

Controlled documents

Contracts, licenses, insurance documents, permits, compliance records, and other important files.

Renewals and expirations

Expiration dates, renewal dates, missing dates, missing owners, and documents that need follow-up.

Operational visibility

Reports, backups, and local license status for organizations that need document control without cloud dependence.

Why it exists

Important documents often fail quietly. A certificate expires, a contract renewal is missed, a vendor record has no clear owner, or a permit sits in the wrong folder until someone needs it urgently.

Vigil is being built to keep those records under control: visible, locally managed, tied to the right accounts and providers, and easier to review before small misses become larger operational problems.

Local-first document control

Vigil is designed for small organizations that want important operational documents closer to their own environment instead of depending on a cloud service for every record, reminder, report, and backup.

  • Keep key business documents visible
  • Track dates, renewals, owners, and missing information
  • Support compliance records and operational follow-up
  • Maintain a local-first approach to document control
