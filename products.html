01. The Engineer

A command center for organized chaos.

For people whose work has become a maze of scripts, reports, files, reminders, and repeatable tasks, The Engineer brings the pieces into one controlled launcher.

This early trial version is being used for testing and feedback. It is currently focused on launching PowerShell-based workflows, with a broader launcher system planned over time.

  • Central launcher for scripts, reports, and task tools
  • Designed for future personal and organizational versions
