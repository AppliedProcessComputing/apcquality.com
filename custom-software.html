Common Questions

What kind of custom software does Applied Process Computing build?

Applied Process Computing builds practical internal tools for workflows, reporting, records, reminders, intake, document tracking, automation, and recurring operational processes.

Do you work with spreadsheets and Excel-based workflows?

Yes. Many projects start with spreadsheets, exports, manual reports, or recurring Excel cleanup. The goal is to reduce fragile manual work without overbuilding.

Do you build local-first software?

Yes, when it fits the workflow. Applied Process Computing prioritizes practical systems that keep data and processes closer to the organization instead of forcing everything into a cloud platform.

Do you work with nonprofits and small businesses?

Yes. Applied Process Computing is built for small businesses, nonprofits, community organizations, and teams that need better systems without unnecessary complexity.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. The first conversation is used to understand the workflow and decide whether custom software, automation, cleanup, an existing product, or no build at all makes sense.
