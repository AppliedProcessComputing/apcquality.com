Who it is for

The Engineer is for offices where recurring computer-based work is scattered across scripts, spreadsheets, shared folders, browser bookmarks, reports, and instructions that only one or two people fully understand.

  • Staff get one clean place to start recurring work.
  • Managers get less process reinvention each time the work repeats.
  • Directors get more consistent execution across repeated workflows.
  • Owners and executives get a small tool that helps reduce scattered workflow risk.
